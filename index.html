TACHIBANA
A Stay That Gives Back.

Nestled in a quiet mountain village, TACHIBANA is a lovingly restored traditional house.

We welcome only one group per day, offering a private retreat where you can truly rest your heart.

Surrounded by the changing seasons, warmed by the hearth, and nourished by local seasonal delicacies,

you will experience moments where time seems to stand still.

With your family or someone special, please enjoy a unique stay found nowhere else.
